🏸 About us

Badminton, community, and live sports content

Michigan Badminton Lovers is a community-driven sports channel and club. We organize local games, film highlights, share tips for all skill levels, and help more people discover how fun badminton can be.

For players

Play more, level up faster

Join regular meetups, friendly matches, and practice sessions. Learn from other players, try new drills, and track your progress.

For fans

Local badminton, on screen

Enjoy match highlights, player spotlights, and behind-the-scenes clips from gyms and tournaments across Michigan.

For beginners

Start where you are

Never played before? No problem. We share simple tutorials and beginner-friendly sessions, so you can learn at your own pace.
